# sleepypod Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

## Data Collection

sleepypod does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers.

All data — including sleep records, heart rate, temperature settings, and sensor readings — is processed and stored locally on your Pod and your iOS device.

## Network Communication

sleepypod communicates exclusively with your Pod over your local WiFi network. No data is sent to the internet, cloud services, or third-party servers.

## Health Data

Sleep stage classification, heart rate, HRV, and breathing rate data is processed entirely on-device. This data is never transmitted off your local network.

If you choose to write sleep data to Apple Health, that data is governed by Apple's Health privacy policies.

## Analytics & Tracking

sleepypod contains no analytics SDKs, crash reporters, or tracking code.
